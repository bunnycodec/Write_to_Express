Forms of writing

Poem : A piece of writing in which the expression of feelings and ideas is given intensity by particular attention to diction

Story : An account of imaginary or real people and events told for entertainment.

Novel : A fictitious prose narrative of book length, typically representing character and action with some degree of realism.

Play : A play is a form of literature written by a playwright, usually consisting of dialogue between characters, intended for theatrical performance rather than just reading.

Letter : A written, typed, or printed communication, sent in an envelope by post or messenger.
